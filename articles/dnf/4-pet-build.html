一、基础科普：宠物、光环、装扮系统全览

I. Basics: Pet, Aura & Outfit System Overview

1.1 宠物系统

1.1 Pet System

DNF手游宠物分为四个品级：普通 → 高级 → 稀有 → 史诗。毕业级宠物目前唯一来源是春节礼包（每年更新一次），属性碾压其他所有渠道。非春节期间的替代方案包括：活动赠送宠物（属性约为春节宠物的85%-90%）、战令宠物（约80%-85%）和抽卡/勋章兑换宠物（约75%-80%）。宠物还涉及宠物装备（红/蓝/绿三个槽位）和宠物技能洗练两大子系统。

DNF Mobile pets are divided into four grades: Normal → Advanced → Rare → Epic. The sole source of endgame pets is currently the Lunar New Year pack (updated annually), with stats crushing all other sources. Off-season alternatives include: event-gifted pets (~85%-90% of Lunar New Year pet stats), Battle Pass pets (~80%-85%), and gacha/medal exchange pets (~75%-80%). Pets also involve two subsystems: pet equipment (red/blue/green slots) and pet skill rerolling.

1.2 光环系统

1.2 Aura System

光环主要来源是国庆礼包和大型活动（如周年庆）。国庆光环提供1-50级技能+1的属性加成，是DNF手游中提升最大的单一光环。活动光环通常属性约为国庆光环的70%-80%。光环的属性差异直接反映在角色面板上，一个国庆光环比活动光环多提升约5%-8%的总伤害。

Auras primarily come from National Day packs and major events (e.g., anniversary). The National Day aura provides +1 to all Lv. 1-50 skills — the single largest aura boost in DNF Mobile. Event auras typically have ~70%-80% of National Day aura stats. Aura stat differences directly reflect on character panels — a National Day aura provides ~5%-8% more total damage than an event aura.

1.3 装扮（时装）与天空套

1.3 Outfits (Avatars) & Rare Avatar Sets

装扮系统分为三层：普通/高级时装 → 稀有装扮（天空套） → 神器装扮（龙袍）。普通时装仅提供外观，高级时装提供基础属性（约3%-5%提升）。天空套是大部分玩家的毕业装扮目标，提供约8%-12%的综合提升（含套装效果和城镇移动速度）。神器装扮（龙袍）属性在天空套基础上再提升约3%，但成本是天空套的2-3倍，性价比极低。

The outfit system has three tiers: Normal/Advanced Avatars → Rare Avatars (Sky Set) → Legendary Avatars (Dragon Robe). Normal avatars are cosmetic only; Advanced avatars provide base stats (~3%-5% boost). Rare Avatars are most players' endgame target, providing ~8%-12% total boost (including set effects and town movement speed). Legendary Avatars add ~3% on top of Rare Avatars but cost 2-3x more — extremely poor value.

三、档位方案：各预算的毕业路线规划

III. Budget Plans: Endgame Roadmap by Spending Tier

零氪党（¥0）

F2P ($0)

零氪玩家可以获取到：活动赠送宠物（每年约2-3只可用级别的宠物）、周年庆/春节活动光环、通过活动送的合成器碎片慢慢攒天空套（周期约8-12个月）。核心策略是囤泰拉在拍卖行买可交易的毕业装备。春节宠物无法直接获取（绑定春节套），但可以通过拍卖行购买已开封的春节称号来部分弥补。

F2P players can obtain: event-gifted pets (~2-3 usable pets per year), anniversary/Lunar New Year event auras, and slowly accumulate Rare Avatars via event synthesizer fragments (~8-12 month cycle). Core strategy: hoard Terra to buy tradable endgame gear on Auction House. Lunar New Year pets cannot be obtained directly (bound to pack), but you can partially compensate by buying opened Lunar New Year titles from Auction House.

微氪党（¥29.9-69.9/月）

Low Spender ($4.99-$9.99/month)

微氪玩家最优路线：春节套1套（¥358）= 毕业宠物 + 毕业称号 + 8件时装 + 宝珠。这是囊括宠物、称号和基础时装的最高效单笔消费。有了春节套的8件时装后，用活动送的合成器碎片慢慢合天空即可。光环方面先用活动光环过渡，等国庆时再决定是否购入。

Optimal low-spender route: 1 Lunar New Year pack ($49.99) = endgame pet + endgame title + 8 avatars + beads. This is the single most efficient purchase covering pet, title, and base outfits. With the 8 avatars from the Lunar New Year pack, slowly synthesize Rare Avatars using event-given synthesizer fragments. For auras, use event auras as a stopgap and decide on National Day pack later.

中氪党（¥108-328/月）

Mid Spender ($15-$45/month)

毕业路线：春节1套（宠物+称号）+ 国庆1套（光环）+ 天空套（活动合成法，约¥498-598）。全年核心消费约¥1198-1398，即可获得除龙袍外的全套毕业外观/宠物/光环。天空套的合成策略：趁活动期使用普通合成器+活动送的合成器碎片，每件天空的期望花费可控制在¥68-85（远低于黄金合成器的¥148-198/件）。

Endgame route: 1 Lunar New Year (pet+title) + 1 National Day (aura) + Rare Avatar (event synthesis, ~$70-$85). Annual core spending ~$170-$200 for full endgame appearance/pet/aura excluding Legendary Avatar. Rare Avatar synthesis strategy: use normal synthesizers + event-given fragments during events to control per-piece expected cost to $8.99-$11.99 (far below golden synthesizer cost of $21-$28/piece).

重氪党（¥498+/月）

Whale ($70+/month)

毕业路线：春节2套（至尊宠物）+ 国庆2套 + 天空套（活动合成法或黄金合成器）+ 考虑龙袍升级。但即使是重氪，龙袍的性价比依然很低——建议只在活动送大量升级材料时再补齐。

Endgame route: 2 Lunar New Year packs (supreme pet) + 2 National Day packs + Rare Avatar (event or golden synthesis) + consider Legendary Avatar upgrade. But even for whales, Legendary Avatar has terrible value — recommended only when events give massive upgrade materials.

四、避坑总结：宠物/光环/装扮最常见的6个花钱误区

IV. Pitfall Summary: 6 Most Common Pet/Aura/Outfit Spending Mistakes

  1. 误区一：用黄金合成器直接合天空套——黄金合成器单价¥12.9-25/个，8件套需¥1198-1398。而普通合成器活动期单价¥5-8，配合活动送的成功率加成和合成器碎片，8件套的实际花费可控制在¥498-598。差距高达2-3倍。
  2. Myth 1: Using golden synthesizers directly for Rare Avatars — Golden synthesizers cost $1.99-$3.49 each; an 8-piece set costs $170-$200. Normal synthesizers during events cost $0.79-$1.19 each, and with event success rate bonuses and free fragments, an 8-piece set can be done for $70-$85. That's a 2-3x difference.
  3. 误区二：宠物技能硬洗完美——宠物技能品级调整每洗一次消耗金币和材料，洗出完美三属性的概率极低（估算低于3%）。非重氪玩家洗出两个核心属性即可收手，追求完美三属性的边际成本极高（可能多花¥198-498）。
  4. Myth 2: Brute-forcing perfect pet skills — Each pet skill reroll consumes gold and materials; the probability of perfect triple attributes is extremely low (estimated <3%). Non-whales should stop after getting two core attributes. Chasing perfect triple attributes has extreme marginal cost (potentially $28-$70 extra).
  5. 误区三：在非春节/国庆期间高价买光环或宠物——拍卖行在春节/国庆期间毕业宠物的可交易版本价格最低（大量玩家出售回血）。非节日期间价格可能翻倍。如果你错过礼包购买，最佳策略是等下一个节日礼包发售期再在拍卖行大量扫货。
  6. Myth 3: Buying auras or pets at high prices outside holiday seasons — Auction House prices for tradable endgame pet/aura items are lowest during Lunar New Year/National Day (players mass-sell to recoup costs). Off-season prices can double. If you missed the pack purchase, the best strategy is to wait for the next holiday pack launch window and buy in bulk from Auction House.
  7. 误区四：战令时装直接分解——战令赠送的时装虽然属性一般，但可以用作天空套合成的"胚子"。每件战令时装相当于省下了一件合成所需的普通时装（¥12.9-25/件）。8件就是¥108-198的节省。
  8. Myth 4: Dismantling Battle Pass avatars — Battle Pass avatars, while having average stats, can serve as "fodder" for Rare Avatar synthesis. Each BP avatar saves the cost of a normal avatar needed for synthesis ($1.79-$3.49/piece). 8 pieces = $15-$28 saved.
  9. 误区五：错过春节套后随便买个活动宠凑合——活动宠与春节宠的伤害差距约8%-12%，这个差距在团本里等于少一件+11装备。如果你的预算有限错过了一年，下一年的春节套优先级必须排到最高。
  10. Myth 5: Settling for a random event pet after missing Lunar New Year pack — The damage gap between event pets and Lunar New Year pets is ~8%-12% — equivalent to missing a +11 gear piece in raids. If you missed a year due to budget, the next year's Lunar New Year pack must be your absolute top priority.
  11. 误区六：龙袍是"必须追的终极目标"——龙袍比天空套多约3%的属性提升，但成本是天空套的3-5倍。这笔钱投在装备强化上（例如从+12冲到+13）的提升远大于龙袍。龙袍的真正受众是已经全身+13/14且没有其他提升渠道的顶尖玩家。
  12. Myth 6: Legendary Avatar is the "must-chase ultimate goal" — Legendary Avatar gives ~3% more stats than Rare Avatar but costs 3-5x more. That money invested in gear enhancement (e.g., +12 to +13) yields far greater gains. Legendary Avatars are truly for top players who already have full +13/+14 with no other upgrade channels.
