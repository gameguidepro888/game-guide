一、基础科普：角色抽卡优先级的评估框架1. Primer: Character Pull Priority Evaluation Framework

1.1 评估维度体系1.1 Evaluation Dimensions

判断一个角色是否值得用珍贵的原石去抽取，需要从四个维度综合评估：Determining whether a character is worth your precious Primogems requires evaluating four dimensions:

  • 泛用性Versatility：角色能适配多少不同队伍？辅助/副C的泛用性通常远高于主C（主C只能服务一种队伍，辅助可以服务任何队伍）。: How many different teams can this character fit into? Support/sub-DPS characters are almost always far more versatile than main DPS characters (a main DPS only serves one team, while a support can serve any team).
  • 保值度Future-Proofing：角色在后续版本中被"环境淘汰"或"数值膨胀淘汰"的概率。七神因其元素机制内核，大概率长期保值。: The probability this character gets pushed out by meta shifts or power creep in future patches. Archons are highly likely to remain relevant long-term because their kits are rooted in core elemental mechanics.
  • 伤害/辅助天花板Damage/Support Ceiling：角色在同等投入下的上限表现。: How high the character's ceiling is at equal investment levels.
  • 低投入友好度Low-Investment Friendliness：角色在低命/低配（四星武器、一般圣遗物）下就能发挥多大作用。这个维度对于低氪玩家最为关键。: How much value the character delivers at low constellations and with budget gear (4-star weapons, average artifacts). This dimension is the most critical for low spenders.

核心原则Core Principle：辅助 > 副C > 主C。辅助可以为你所有的队伍提供价值，一个好辅助（如钟离的盾、万叶的增伤减抗）能让你的任何主C都表现更好。而主C换一个就意味着一整套配队需要重建。: Support > Sub-DPS > Main DPS. A support provides value to all your teams — a great support (e.g., Zhongli's shield, Kazuha's damage buff and resistance shred) makes any main DPS perform better. Swapping main DPS, on the other hand, means rebuilding an entire team from scratch.

1.2 七神/龙王级角色 —— 必抽档1.2 Archon/Sovereign-Tier Characters — Must-Pull

以下角色在原神体系中属于"闭眼抽不亏"级别。他们的技能机制深入到了元素反应和队伍构建的核心层面，几乎不可能被完全淘汰：The following characters are in the "can't go wrong pulling them" tier. Their kits are deeply intertwined with elemental reactions and team-building at the most fundamental level — they are nearly impossible to fully power creep:

  • 钟离（岩神）Zhongli (Geo Archon)：最强护盾，持续时间大于冷却时间，自带全元素抗性削减20%。钟离的存在让游戏的难度曲线下沉了一个大档位——有钟离和没钟离是两种游戏体验。0命即可，无需追命座。: The strongest shield in the game, with duration longer than cooldown, plus 20% universal resistance shred. Zhongli's presence shifts the game's difficulty curve by an entire tier — playing with Zhongli and playing without him are two completely different experiences. C0 is all you need; no constellation chasing required.
  • 雷电将军（雷神）Raiden Shogun (Electro Archon)：全队充能引擎 + 爆发副C。她的元素爆发为全队回复能量，让她成为几乎所有依赖大招的队伍的核心组件。关键命座是C2（无视60%防御），但C0已经足够强。: Team-wide energy battery + burst sub-DPS. Her Elemental Burst restores energy for the entire team, making her a core component of nearly any burst-reliant team. Her key constellation is C2 (ignores 60% of enemy DEF), but C0 is already very strong.
  • 纳西妲（草神）Nahida (Dendro Archon)：草反应的核心。在须弥版本后，草元素相关的所有强力反应（激化、绽放、燃烧）都离不开纳西妲的持续草元素附着和精通BUFF。0命即可，C2对部分队伍有质的飞跃。: The core of all Dendro reactions. After the Sumeru patches, every powerful Dendro-based reaction (Quicken, Bloom, Burning) relies on Nahida's sustained Dendro application and EM buff. C0 is sufficient; C2 provides a qualitative leap for certain teams.
  • 芙宁娜（水神）Furina (Hydro Archon)：全队增伤 + 治疗/输出双形态。她的元素战技提供持续后台输出和全队扣血/治疗机制，元素爆发提供基于生命值变化的巨额增伤BUFF（叠满约75-100%增伤）。是目前最强的增伤辅助之一。: Team-wide DMG buff + healing/DPS dual stance. Her Elemental Skill provides sustained off-field damage and a team HP-drain/healing mechanic, while her Elemental Burst grants a massive DMG% buff based on HP fluctuations (up to ~75-100% DMG bonus when fully stacked). One of the strongest DMG-buffing supports in the game.
  • 万叶（枫原万叶）Kazuha (Kaedehara Kazuha)：虽然不是神，但聚怪+元素增伤+减抗的组合让他成为事实上的"必抽"。只要你的队伍需要元素伤害，万叶就能提供约35-40%的伤害增幅。: Though not an Archon, his combination of crowd control + elemental DMG buff + resistance shred makes him a de facto "must-pull." Any team that deals elemental damage can expect roughly 35-40% more damage with Kazuha.

四、避坑总结：角色抽卡的5大常见错误4. Pitfall Roundup: Top 5 Character Pulling Mistakes

  1. "这个角色是T0，我必须抽""This character is T0, I have to pull"——T0排名基于 — T0 rankings are based on 当前深渊环境和高配队伍the current Abyss cycle and high-investment teams，这两个条件你未必满足。一个T0角色在你手里的表现可能不如一个T1但适合你现有角色池的角色。判断标准改为："这个角色能不能让我的主力队伍变得更强？"而非"这个角色在榜单上排第几？" — two conditions you may not meet. A T0 character in your hands could perform worse than a T1 character that synergizes with your existing roster. Change your evaluation criteria to: "Will this character make my main team stronger?" rather than "Where does this character rank on a tier list?"
  2. "命座提升30%，好划算""30% constellation boost, what a deal"——30%听起来很多，但你需要额外花75-150抽（12,000-24,000原石）来获得。同样的原石去抽一个新角色，提升的往往是"队伍选择的多样性×100%"。除非你对该角色的喜爱度超越了"理性决策"的范畴，否则命座不值得。 — 30% sounds great, but it costs an extra 75-150 pulls (12,000-24,000 Primogems). Spend those same Primogems on a new character and the gain is often "team-building diversity x 100%." Unless your love for that character transcends rational decision-making, constellations simply aren't worth it.
  3. "常驻出金了，这个角色一定很强""I got a standard 5-star — this character must be strong"——迪卢克曾是开服最强，刻晴曾是雷系扛把子，但到了2026年，常驻五星在限定角色的压制下已经全面退居二线。把获取到的常驻五星视为"合格的主C/副C"而非"毕业级主力"，调整期望避免失望。 — Diluc was once the strongest at launch, Keqing was the Electro queen, but by 2026, standard-banner 5-stars have been thoroughly pushed to the sidelines by limited characters. Treat any standard 5-star you pull as a "serviceable main/sub-DPS" rather than an "endgame carry" — adjust expectations to avoid disappointment.
  4. "四星满命随便抽，总会有用的""I'll just pull for 4-star constellations, they'll come in handy"——在非UP池子里硬捞四星是非常低效的行为。4星UP角色的出货率并不高（约13%概率出UP四星之一），你可能花30-40抽是为了一个四星，结果不小心把保底提前触发在了你不想要的五星上面。 — Fishing for 4-stars on a non-rate-up banner is highly inefficient. The rate for a specific rate-up 4-star is not high (roughly 13% chance per 4-star pull), and you could spend 30-40 pulls chasing a 4-star only to accidentally trigger your 5-star pity on a character you don't want. 绝不要为了四星去抽你不想要五星的卡池Never pull on a 5-star banner you don't want just to get the 4-stars on it.
  5. "下版本好像有更强的角色，这版本全跳""Next patch might have a stronger character, I'll skip everything"——"下一个更强"是永无止境的等待。角色复刻周期通常为6-12个月，跳过钟离可能需要等将近一年。合理的做法是：优先保底自己最需要的神级角色，然后留一部分原石给新角色的评测。不要因为"害怕错过更强的"而一直不抽。 — "The next one might be stronger" is an endless waiting game. Character rerun cycles are typically 6-12 months; skipping Zhongli could mean waiting nearly a year. The sensible approach: secure the Archon-tier characters you need most first, then keep a Primogem reserve for evaluating new characters. Don't keep skipping because you're "afraid of missing out on something stronger."
