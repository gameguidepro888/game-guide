1.2 定轨系统（Epitomized Path）详解1.2 Epitomized Path Explained

定轨是武器池的核心保护机制：你在抽卡前选择一把"目标武器"，每次出金如果不是你选的目标武器，就会获得1点"命定值"（Fate Point）。The Epitomized Path is the weapon banner's core safety net: before pulling, you select a "target weapon." Every time you pull a 5-star that isn't your target, you earn 1 Fate Point. 累计2点命定值后，下一次出金100%是你定的目标武器。Once you accumulate 2 Fate Points, your next 5-star is 100% guaranteed to be your chosen weapon. 最大抽数 = 2次命定值积累（最多2×80=160抽）+ 1次保底出目标（最多80抽）= Maximum pulls = 2 Fate Point accumulations (max 2x80=160 pulls) + 1 guaranteed target pity (max 80 pulls) = 最多240抽240 pulls maximum.

但在现实中，240抽是极端情况。实际数学模型如下：In reality, 240 pulls is the extreme tail case. The actual mathematical model:

  • 50%玩家的抽数Median pulls（中位数）：约 (50th percentile): roughly 130-150抽130-150 pulls
  • 10%倒霉玩家的抽数10% unluckiest players：接近200-240抽: close to 200-240 pulls

需要特别注意的是：Crucially: 命定值在每个武器池之间不继承Fate Points do NOT carry over between weapon banners. 也就是说，如果你在一个武器池里攒了1点命定值但没有继续抽到出目标，池子结束后你的命定值将归零。这是武器池最大的"沉没成本陷阱"——要么一次抽到底，要么之前攒的命定值全部白费。In other words, if you accumulate 1 Fate Point on a weapon banner but don't continue pulling until you get your target, those Fate Points reset to zero when the banner ends. This is the weapon banner's biggest "sunk-cost trap" — either commit all the way, or watch your accumulated Fate Points go to waste.

四、避坑总结：武器池最常见的5个错误4. Pitfall Roundup: The 5 Most Common Weapon Banner Mistakes

  1. "我角色都齐了就差武器了，必须抽""I've got all the characters, just need the weapon — must pull"——先检查你是否真的需要这把武器。专武的提升在多数情况下是15-25%的伤害增幅，而你的队伍可能还有其他更高效的提升方式（如圣遗物优化、配队调整、操作练习）。240抽的价值远大于15%的伤害提升。 — First check whether you truly need that weapon. A signature weapon's improvement is 15-25% more damage in most cases, and your team likely has other, more efficient upgrade paths (artifact optimization, team adjustments, mechanical practice). The value of 240 pulls far exceeds a 15% damage increase.
  2. "命定值攒了1点不能浪费""I've got 1 Fate Point, can't let it go to waste"——这是武器池的终极陷阱。如果你已经花了130抽攒了1点命定值但没有出货，继续追可能需要再花50-110抽。这些原石足够你去角色池拿一个0命五星。关键建议： — This is the ultimate weapon banner trap. If you've spent 130 pulls to accumulate 1 Fate Point without hitting your target, continuing could cost another 50-110 pulls. Those Primogems are enough to get a C0 5-star from the character banner. Key advice: 在开始抽武器池之前就决定好是否愿意走完定轨。不要下了池子之后才"边走边看"Decide whether you're willing to finish the Epitomized Path BEFORE you start pulling. Don't "play it by ear" after you've already started.
  3. "四星武器太丑/太弱，必须用五星""4-star weapons look bad / are too weak, I need 5-stars"——外观的确是武器池的隐藏价值（武器和角色搭配的视觉效果），但你需要为"好看"支付约¥1,398-2,098的机会成本。如果外观对你的游戏体验至关重要，那就当成"买时装"的心态——原石 = 购买外观体验的货币。这不是错误，但需要你对花费有清醒的认知。 — Aesthetics are indeed the hidden value of the weapon banner (the visual synergy between weapon and character), but you're paying roughly $200-300 in opportunity cost for "looking good." If aesthetics are crucial to your experience, treat it with a "buying a skin" mindset — Primogems = currency for purchasing visual experience. This isn't a mistake per se, but you need to be crystal clear about what you're paying for.
  4. "这把武器泛用性高，什么角色都能用""This weapon is versatile, any character can use it"——所谓的"泛用武器"（如天空系列）实际上对每个特定角色的提升都不如专武。一把泛用武器可能在10个角色上都能用，但对每个角色只提升10-15%，而一把专武对一个角色提升25-30%。如果你不玩那个专武对应角色，泛用武器仍然不能让你获得应有的回报。 — So-called "versatile weapons" (like the Skyward series) actually underperform compared to signature weapons on every specific character. A versatile weapon may be usable on 10 characters but only provides a 10-15% boost on each, while a signature weapon gives 25-30% on its intended user. If you don't play that signature weapon's character, the versatile weapon still won't give you the returns you're hoping for.
  5. "武器池保底和角色池一样跨池继承""Weapon banner pity carries over just like the character banner"——武器池的保底计数 — Weapon banner pity count 跨池继承DOES carry over across banners，但, but 命定值（定轨进度）不继承Fate Points (Path progress) do NOT carry over。这两者需要严格区分：你在武器池A抽了50抽没出金，到武器池B时保底计数从51开始（继承）；但你在武器池A攒的1点命定值在武器池B会归零（不继承）。混淆这两者是大量玩家的认知盲区。. These two must be strictly distinguished: if you did 50 pulls on Weapon Banner A without a 5-star, on Weapon Banner B your pity counter starts at 51 (carries over); but if you earned 1 Fate Point on Weapon Banner A, it resets to zero on Weapon Banner B (does NOT carry over). Confusing these two concepts is a blind spot for a huge number of players.
