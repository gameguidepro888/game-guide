一、基础科普：荣耀水晶获取机制完全拆解

1. Basics: Glory Crystal Acquisition Mechanics Fully Explained

1.1 积分夺宝是什么？

1.1 What is Points Treasure Hunt?

积分夺宝是王者荣耀的高级抽奖系统，玩家使用荣耀积分进行抽奖，每次抽奖有机会获得荣耀水晶。荣耀水晶可兑换荣耀典藏皮肤（目前共约15款），以及武则天英雄和倪克斯神谕等稀有道具。

Points Treasure Hunt is Honor of Kings' premium draw system. Players use Glory Points to draw, with each draw having a chance to yield a Glory Crystal. Glory Crystals can be exchanged for Glory Collection skins (currently around 15 total), the hero武则天 (Wu Zetian), and rare items like Nyx Divine Oracle.

1.2 积分价格与获取方式

1.2 Point Prices and Acquisition Methods

  • 单抽：60荣耀积分/次
  • Single draw: 60 Glory Points per draw
  • 五连抽：270荣耀积分/次（相当于54积分/抽，9折）
  • 5-draw bundle: 270 Glory Points (equivalent to 54 points/draw, 10% off)
  • 积分购买价格：60积分 = 60点券 = ¥6（标准价）
  • Point purchase price: 60 Points = 60 Coupons = $0.99 (standard rate)
  • 打折时段：每日首次五连抽可用200积分。不定期活动期间五连抽仅需200积分（约74折）
  • Discount periods: First daily 5-draw costs only 200 points. During irregular events, all 5-draws cost 200 points (about 26% off)
  • 积分来源：充值、战令奖励、活动赠送、每日任务（微量）
  • Point sources: Top-ups, Battle Pass rewards, event giveaways, daily quests (small amounts)

2.2 每日抽奖策略——省钱的关键

2.2 Daily Draw Strategy — The Key to Saving Money

荣耀积分夺宝有一个被很多玩家忽略的机制：每日首次五连抽仅需200积分（原价270，省70积分）。如果你不着急，每天只抽1次五连（200积分），那么361抽需要：

The Points Treasure Hunt has a mechanic many players overlook: the first daily 5-draw costs only 200 points (down from 270, saving 70 points each time). If you're not in a rush, drawing just one 5-draw (200 points) per day means 361 draws requires:

  • 361÷5≈73天（约2.5个月）
  • 361÷5≈73 days (about 2.5 months)
  • 总花费：72次×200积分 + 最后一次单抽60积分 = 14,460积分 = ¥1,446
  • Total cost: 72×200 points + final single draw 60 points = 14,460 points = $215
  • 相比一次性抽完（¥1,949），省了¥503，约26%
  • Compared to drawing all at once ($290), you save $75 — about 26%

这是目前公认的最省钱抽水晶方式。缺点是耗时长，适合不着急的玩家。如果你能等到积分夺宝打折活动（通常春节、周年庆期间），叠加每日首次五连折扣，成本可以进一步压缩到 ¥1,200以下。

This is the widely acknowledged cheapest way to draw crystals. The downside is the time required — it suits patient players. If you can wait for Points Treasure Hunt discount events (usually during Chinese New Year and Anniversary), and stack them with the daily first 5-draw discount, costs can be pushed under $180.

2.3 什么时候抽最划算？

2.3 When Is the Best Time to Draw?

  • 春节/周年庆（最佳）：积分夺宝通常有全时段折扣（五连200积分），每日首次五连可能更低。同时伴随充值返利活动，充指定金额额外赠送积分。
  • Chinese New Year / Anniversary (best): Points Treasure Hunt typically offers all-day discounts (200 pts per 5-draw), with daily first 5-draw possibly even cheaper. These also coincide with top-up rebate events that grant bonus points for hitting thresholds.
  • 五五开黑节（较好）：通常有积分夺宝折扣 + 特定典藏皮肤限时上架/返场。
  • May 5th Festival (good): Usually features Points Treasure Hunt discounts plus limited-time release/return of specific Collection skins.
  • 新典藏上架首周：新皮肤上架时通常伴随积分夺宝活动，但折扣力度一般不如春节。
  • New Collection skin launch week: New skin releases usually come with Treasure Hunt events, but discounts are generally weaker than CNY offerings.
  • 日常积攒（最省但最慢）：每天只抽1次五连（200积分），利用战令和活动赠送的积分慢慢攒。适合有耐心的玩家。
  • Daily accumulation (cheapest but slowest): Only do the daily first 5-draw (200 points), slowly accumulating from Battle Pass and event point giveaways. Best for patient players.

As of May 2026, there are approximately 15 Glory Collection skins. Since each crystal costs a substantial amount ($130-$290), choosing which one to exchange is critically important. Below are ranked by overall recommendation (detailed ratings in member-exclusive content):

3.2 一颗荣耀水晶值不值 ¥1,950？

3.2 Is One Glory Crystal Worth $250?

这个问题需要拆开看：如果你追求最优性价比，¥1,950可以购买约11款首周折扣传说皮肤，或50个赛季的进阶战令。单从皮肤数量看，典藏确实溢价严重。但从稀缺性和身份象征角度看，典藏皮肤是王者荣耀最高品质的代表，且水晶兑换是唯一获取途径（不会直售、不会进碎片商店）。结论：如果你是收藏党或特定英雄的深度玩家，典藏值得；如果你是性价比党，建议把预算花在战令和限定皮肤上。

This question requires unpacking: if you pursue optimal value, $250 can buy about 11 first-week-discounted legendary skins, or 50 seasons of Advanced Battle Pass. From a pure skin count perspective, Collection skins are indeed heavily overpriced. But from scarcity and status symbol perspectives, Collection skins represent the highest quality tier in Honor of Kings, and crystal exchange is the only way to obtain them (no direct sale, no fragment shop). Verdict: if you're a collector or dedicated main of a specific hero, Collection skins are worth it; if you are value-oriented, we recommend spending your budget on the Battle Pass and limited skins instead.

四、避坑总结：荣耀水晶抽奖的5大常见误区

4. Pitfall Guide: 5 Common Glory Crystal Draw Myths

  1. 误区一："单抽出奇迹"——单抽每次60积分，五连每次54积分（打折后更低）。除非你只剩最后一次保底，否则永远用五连。单抽比五连贵11%，累计下来差距很大。
  2. Myth #1: "Single draws hit miracles" — Single draws cost 60 points each; 5-draws cost 54 per draw (even less with discounts). Unless you are on your absolute final pity draw, always use 5-draws. Single draws are 11% more expensive, and the difference adds up fast.
  3. 误区二："等官方提高概率再抽"——荣耀水晶的基础概率是固定的，官方从未在特定时段提高基础概率。所谓的"活动期好出"主要是因为打折让你用更少积分抽了更多次，而非概率本身变化。
  4. Myth #2: "Wait for the devs to boost rates before drawing" — The base Glory Crystal probability is fixed; the devs have never raised the base rate during specific periods. The perception that "drops are better during events" comes from discounts letting you draw more times for fewer points, not from probability changes.
  5. 误区三："先随便抽几十次试试运气"——积分夺宝有一个容易被忽略的心理陷阱：一旦开始抽，沉没成本会让你很难停下来。很多玩家只是想"抽50次试试"，结果一路抽到保底。所以开始之前务必设定预算上限。
  6. Myth #3: "Just do a few dozen draws to test my luck" — Points Treasure Hunt has an easily overlooked psychological trap: once you start drawing, sunk cost makes it very hard to stop. Many players intend to "try 50 draws" and end up going all the way to pity. Always set a hard budget cap before you start.
  7. 误区四："武则天必须用水晶换"——武则天确实只能通过荣耀水晶兑换，但她的强度在当前版本（2026年）并非T0级别。如果你不玩法刺，¥1,950换一个仓库英雄非常不划算。先确认你真的会玩武则天再考虑兑换。
  8. Myth #4: "You must use a crystal for Wu Zetian" — Wu Zetian is indeed only obtainable via Glory Crystal exchange, but her strength in the current meta (2026) is not T0-tier. If you don't play mage-assassins, spending $250 on a bench hero is terrible value. Confirm you will actually play her before committing.
  9. 误区五："每天只抽一次五连就是最省的"——虽然每日首次五连200积分确实省，但如果你能等到积分夺宝全时段打折活动（五连统一200积分），那么一天抽多次和分天抽的成本是一样的，可以更快拿到水晶。关键是在活动期间集中抽，而非无活动时每天慢抽。
  10. Myth #5: "One daily 5-draw is always the cheapest" — While the daily first 5-draw at 200 points does save money, if you wait for an all-day Points Treasure Hunt discount event (all 5-draws at 200 points), drawing multiple times in one day costs the same as spreading them out — and you get the crystal faster. The key is drawing during events, not slowly drawing one per day outside of events.
