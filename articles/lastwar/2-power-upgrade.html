1.1 四大战力维度的真实权重1.1 True Weight of the Four Power Dimensions

Last War的"总战力"数值由四大维度组成，但它们的Last War's "total power" value comprises four dimensions, but their 战场实际影响力actual battlefield impact与数值权重不完全一致：does not perfectly align with their numerical weight:

  • 英雄（Heroes）—— 40%战斗力贡献Heroes — 40% Combat Contribution：包括英雄星级、等级、装备、技能等级。UR英雄从3星到5星的战力跃升最为显著（约35%提升）。这是整个游戏中最值得投资的维度。: Includes hero star level, level, gear, and skill level. The power jump from 3-star to 5-star for UR heroes is the most significant (approximately 35% increase). This is the single most worthwhile investment dimension in the entire game.
  • 科研（Research）—— 25%战斗力贡献Research — 25% Combat Contribution：科技树中的兵种攻防加成、行军速度、资源产量等。科研的特点是"时间换战力"——投入周期长，但回报永久且影响所有部队。: Includes troop attack/defense bonuses, march speed, resource production, etc. Research is characterized by "time for power" — long investment cycles, but permanent returns that affect all troops.
  • 建筑（Buildings）—— 20%战斗力贡献Buildings — 20% Combat Contribution：HQ总部等级决定解锁内容的上限，是关键门槛。但单纯为了战力数值而升级建筑（不配合科研和英雄）是低效的。: HQ level determines the ceiling for unlocking content and is a critical gate. But upgrading buildings purely for the power number (without matching research and hero investment) is inefficient.
  • 部队（Troops）—— 15%战斗力贡献Troops — 15% Combat Contribution：训练大量低级部队会显著抬高战力数字，但在高级PVP中容易被一波清空。部队数量不等于战斗实力。: Training masses of low-tier troops inflates your power number dramatically, but they get wiped out easily in high-level PvP. Troop count does not equal combat strength.

四、避坑总结：战力提升中最常见的6个方向性错误4. Traps to Avoid: The 6 Most Common Directional Errors in Power Progression

  1. 错误一：用通用加速冲建筑升级Mistake #1: Using General Speedups on buildings——通用加速（General Speedup）是Last War中最珍贵的消耗品，因为它是唯一可以自由用于任何等待时间的加速。如果你用它来加速建筑升级，将来需要科研加速时就会极度短缺。铁律：通用加速只用于科研，建筑升级用专门的Building Speedup或用时间等。 — General Speedups are the most precious consumable in Last War because they're the only Speedup that can be freely applied to any timer. Using them on building upgrades means you'll face extreme shortages when you need research Speedups. Iron rule: General Speedups are for research ONLY. Building upgrades use dedicated Building Speedups or just wait it out.
  2. 错误二：钻石直接购买Speed UpMistake #2: Buying Speedups directly with Diamonds——商店中钻石→Speed Up的兑换比是所有钻石消费中性价比最低的。1小时Speed Up需要约80钻石，而80钻石用于购买VIP点数可以为你每天带来持续的Speed Up产出。永远不要在商店买Speed Up。 — The Diamond-to-Speedup exchange rate in the shop is the worst value of any Diamond spend. 1 hour of Speedup costs about 80 Diamonds, while those same 80 Diamonds spent on VIP Points would generate a continuous daily Speedup stream. Never buy Speedups from the shop.
  3. 错误三：联盟积分买资源Mistake #3: Spending Alliance Coins on resources——联盟商店中金币/粮食/钢材的兑换非常不划算。联盟积分应该严格用于：UR碎片（每周必换） > 传送道具（战争时期需要） > 其他。UR碎片是联盟商店中唯一不可通过其他免费渠道大量获取的核心资源。 — Exchanging for Gold/Food/Steel in the Alliance Shop is extremely poor value. Alliance Coins should be strictly used for: UR shards (must-buy weekly) > Teleports (wartime need) > other. UR shards are the only core resource in the Alliance Shop that cannot be obtained in bulk through other free channels.
  4. 错误四：战力数字崇拜——大量生产低级部队Mistake #4: Power number worship — mass-producing low-tier troops——训练大量T1-T3低级部队可以快速拉升战力数字，但这些部队在PVP中几乎不堪一击。一场大战可能让你损失数十万战力——因为这些低级部队被一波清了。正确的做法是解锁高级兵种后逐步升级替换，而非单纯堆数量。 — Training masses of T1-T3 low-tier troops can rapidly inflate your power number, but these troops are nearly useless in PvP. A single big battle could cost you hundreds of thousands in power — because those low-tier troops get wiped in one wave. The correct approach is to unlock high-tier troops and gradually upgrade/replace, not simply stack quantity.
  5. 错误五：杀敌活动（Kill Event）前没有提前准备Mistake #5: Not preparing ahead of Kill Event——Kill Event期间排名奖励非常丰厚，但很多玩家在活动开始后才发现自己没有足够的部队和加速道具来冲分。提前1-2周开始：囤积训练加速、预留足够的资源用于爆兵、确保医院容量足够、选择正确的攻击目标（低战力但有大量部队的玩家是最佳目标）。 — Kill Event ranking rewards are extremely generous, but many players only realize after the event starts that they lack sufficient troops and Speedups to push for score. Start preparing 1-2 weeks ahead: hoard training Speedups, reserve enough resources for mass troop production, ensure sufficient hospital capacity, and select the right targets (low-power players with large troop counts are optimal targets).
  6. 错误六：忽视联盟科技只点个人科技Mistake #6: Ignoring Alliance Tech, only researching personal tech——联盟科技中的兵种攻防加成是对所有出征部队生效的，其实际战斗力提升远大于个人科技树中的大多数节点。但联盟科技需要联盟积分，这又回到了"联盟积分必须优先用于UR碎片"的矛盾。解决方式：UR碎片和联盟科技贡献轮流进行——每周联盟积分的前50%买UR碎片，后50%捐科技。 — Alliance tech troop ATK/DEF bonuses apply to ALL deployed troops, and their actual combat power gain far exceeds most nodes in the personal tech tree. But Alliance Tech requires Alliance Coins, bringing us back to the "UR shards vs. Alliance Tech" dilemma. Solution: alternate — spend the first 50% of weekly Alliance Coins on UR shards, the remaining 50% on tech donations.
