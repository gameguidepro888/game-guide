四、避坑总结：抽卡系统中最常见的6个浪费行为4. Traps to Avoid: The 6 Most Common Wastes in the Gacha System

  1. 错误一：非活动期使用Premium TicketsMistake #1: Using Premium Tickets outside events——这是Last War中最严重、最常见但也最容易被忽视的资源浪费。活动期间抽卡有：多抽里程碑奖励（如20抽送5张票、40抽送UR碎片等）+ 活动专属UR概率UP + 保底计数。非活动期抽卡等于放弃了所有这些福利，每张票的实际价值缩水60%-70%。 — This is the most severe, most common, yet most easily overlooked resource waste in Last War. Event-period pulls have: multi-pull milestone rewards (e.g., 5 free tickets at 20 pulls, UR shards at 40 pulls, etc.) + event-exclusive UR rate UP + pity tracking. Pulling outside events means giving up all of these benefits, shrinking each ticket's effective value by 60%-70%.
  2. 错误二：以为十连抽只是心理安慰Mistake #2: Thinking 10-pulls are just psychological comfort——十连抽有至少1个SR的保底机制，而10次单抽没有。这意味着十连抽的下限高于单抽十次。除非你距离保底仅剩1-3抽（此时单抽触发保底后切换回十连），否则永远使用十连抽。 — 10-pulls guarantee at least 1 SR, while 10 single pulls do not. This means the floor on a 10-pull is higher than 10 singles. Unless you're only 1-3 pulls from pity (in which case single-pull to trigger pity, then switch back to 10-pull), always use 10-pulls.
  3. 错误三：非主力英雄的UR碎片也去拿Mistake #3: Grabbing UR shards for non-main heroes——联盟商店的UR碎片每周有限购数量（通常每种5-10片）。如果你的目标是让Kimberly从3星升到5星（需要约70片），就应该每周只买Kimberly碎片，跳过其他UR碎片。分散购买会大幅延长主力英雄的成长周期。 — The Alliance Shop has weekly purchase limits on UR shards (typically 5-10 per type). If your goal is to take Kimberly from 3-star to 5-star (roughly 70 shards needed), you should buy ONLY Kimberly shards each week, skipping other UR shards. Spreading purchases dramatically extends your main hero's growth cycle.
  4. 错误四：忽略碎片农场（Shard Farming）Mistake #4: Ignoring shard farming——每天可以通过特定关卡稳定获取SR英雄碎片（每天每个关卡3次，掉落约1-2片）。零氪/微氪玩家应将体力优先投入到关键SR英雄的碎片关卡。一个5星SR可以在中前期提供不亚于3星UR的战斗力。 — You can reliably farm SR hero shards from specific stages daily (3 attempts per stage, approximately 1-2 shard drops). F2P/low spenders should prioritize stamina on key SR hero shard stages. A 5-star SR can provide combat power comparable to a 3-star UR in the early-to-mid game.
  5. 错误五：在保底重置后立刻停止抽卡Mistake #5: Stopping pulls immediately after pity resets——触发保底后计数器归零，但如果你距离下一个多抽里程碑奖励（如40抽的奖励）只差5-10抽，补上这几抽是划算的。反之，如果刚触发保底且距离下个里程碑还很远，应该停手等下次活动。 — After triggering pity, the counter resets to zero. But if you're only 5-10 pulls away from the next multi-pull milestone reward (e.g., the 40-pull reward), topping up those few pulls is worthwhile. Conversely, if you just hit pity and the next milestone is far away, stop and save for the next event.
  6. 错误六：忽视英雄觉醒（Hero Awakening）系统Mistake #6: Ignoring the Hero Awakening system——从Season 6开始，5星以上的英雄可以通过觉醒继续提升（6星/7星）。觉醒需要大量专属碎片（6星需要额外50片，7星需要额外100片）。在计划主力英雄的培养路线时，要预留觉醒的碎片需求——5星不是终点。 — Starting from Season 6, heroes above 5-star can be further upgraded through Awakening (6-star/7-star). Awakening requires a massive number of exclusive shards (6-star needs 50 additional shards, 7-star needs 100 additional shards). When planning your main hero's development roadmap, reserve shards for Awakening needs — 5-star is not the finish line.
