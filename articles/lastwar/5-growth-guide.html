1.1 核心养成维度1.1 Core Progression Dimensions

Last War的养成体系围绕Last War's progression system revolves around two main threads: 英雄成长hero growth和 and 基地发展base development两条主线展开。英雄系统包括：星级（★1-★7）、等级、装备、技能等级。基地系统包括：HQ等级、建筑等级、科研树、部队训练。两条主线相互依赖——HQ等级解锁英雄等级上限，英雄战力决定PVP和活动成绩，而活动成绩又反哺资源和加速道具。理解这个循环是制定养成策略的基础。. The hero system includes: star level (1★-7★), level, gear, and skill level. The base system includes: HQ level, building levels, research tree, and troop training. The two threads are interdependent — HQ level unlocks hero level caps, hero power determines PvP and event performance, and event performance in turn feeds back resources and Speedups. Understanding this cycle is the foundation for building a progression strategy.

四、避坑总结：养成中最常见的6个路线错误4. Traps to Avoid: The 6 Most Common Progression Route Mistakes

  1. 错误一：零氪玩家不加入联盟/加入不活跃联盟Mistake #1: F2P players not joining an alliance / joining an inactive one——联盟是Last War中最被零氪和微氪玩家低估的资源引擎。一个Top 10联盟每周通过联盟商店（UR碎片）、联盟活动（钻石+加速）、联盟帮助（建筑/科研加速）提供的总价值约相当于¥148-210的付费内容。加入死盟等于主动放弃了你最大的免费资源来源。 — Alliance is the most underestimated resource engine for F2P and low spenders in Last War. A Top 10 alliance provides total value equivalent to roughly $20-30 in paid content weekly through the Alliance Shop (UR shards), alliance events (Diamonds + Speedups), and alliance help (building/research Speedups). Joining a dead alliance means voluntarily giving up your largest free resource source.
  2. 错误二：微氪玩家买Monthly Pass但忘领每日奖励Mistake #2: Low spenders buying Monthly Pass but forgetting daily claims——月卡的每日奖励需要手动领取。如果你有一天忘记登录，那一天的100钻石+60分钟加速+100 VIP点数就没了（不补发）。设置每日提醒是月卡玩家的基本功。 — Monthly Pass daily rewards require manual claiming. If you forget to log in for a day, that day's 100 Diamonds + 60 min Speedup + 100 VIP Points are gone (no retroactive grant). Setting a daily reminder is a fundamental habit for Monthly Pass holders.
  3. 错误三：中氪玩家追第三建筑队列过早Mistake #3: Mid spenders buying Third Builder too early——Third Builder（¥358）虽然强大，但在资源有限的新手期，三队列同时开工会导致资源快速枯竭。建议在HQ 20级以上且资源产量稳定后再考虑第三队列。前中期双队列+稳健的资源管理已经足够。 — Third Builder ($49.99) is powerful, but during the resource-scarce early game, running three queues simultaneously will rapidly deplete your resources. It's recommended to consider the Third Builder only after HQ 20+ when resource production is stable. Dual queues + steady resource management is sufficient for the early-to-mid game.
  4. 错误四：所有档位的玩家都平均培养英雄Mistake #4: All budget players evenly leveling heroes——"雨露均沾"式英雄培养是养成效率的最低效方式。无论你在哪个档位，都应该严格遵循：主力DPS先到当前能力上限 → 坦克跟随 → 其他英雄只升到需要的技能解锁等级即可。集中资源突破一个英雄的收益远大于平均分配。 — The "spread the love" approach to hero development is the most inefficient way to progress. Regardless of your spending tier, you should strictly follow: main DPS to current cap first → tank follows → other heroes only to the level needed for skill unlocks. The returns from focused resource investment in one hero vastly exceed equal distribution.
  5. 错误五：忽视英雄装备和技能等级Mistake #5: Neglecting hero gear and skill levels——英雄的专属装备和技能等级对战力的影响常被新手低估。一个装备全金+技能全满的4星UR，其实际战力可能超过一个装备稀烂+技能没点的5星UR。在冲星级的同时，不要忘记装备和技能这两个"战力乘区"。 — The impact of hero exclusive gear and skill levels on combat power is often underestimated by new players. A 4-star UR with full gold gear and maxed skills can actually outperform a 5-star UR with poor gear and unleveled skills. While pushing star levels, don't forget gear and skills — these are your "power multipliers."
  6. 错误六：Season 6 Hero Awakening的投入时机不对Mistake #6: Wrong timing for Season 6 Hero Awakening investment——觉醒系统（6星/7星）消耗极其巨大——6星需要额外50个专属碎片+大量觉醒材料，7星需要额外100个碎片。在只有一个UR到达5星时就冲觉醒是不明智的——先让2-3个核心UR都到5星，再考虑觉醒。觉醒的边际收益（5星→6星约12%提升）远不如将第二个UR从3星拉到5星（约40%提升）。 — The Awakening system (6-star/7-star) is extremely expensive — 6-star requires 50 additional exclusive shards + massive awakening materials; 7-star requires 100 additional shards. Rushing Awakening when you only have one UR at 5-star is unwise — get 2-3 core URs to 5-star first, then consider Awakening. The marginal gain from Awakening (5★ → 6★, roughly 12% increase) is far lower than bringing a second UR from 3-star to 5-star (roughly 40% increase).
