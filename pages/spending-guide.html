充值避坑指南

🔴 限时礼包 ≠ 限时优惠Limited-Time Pack ≠ Limited-Time Deal

很多标注"限时"的礼包其实是常驻礼包的变体，价格和内容与日常无异。务必对比历史价格再决定。Many packs labeled "limited time" are just variants of regular packs with identical pricing and content. Always compare historical prices before buying.

🔴 概率叠加 ≠ 必出Cumulative Probability ≠ Guaranteed Drop

每次抽取的概率是独立的，"抽了50次下次必出"是典型赌徒谬误。看清保底机制和真实期望值。Each pull's probability is independent. "I've pulled 50 times so next one must hit" is a classic gambler's fallacy. Understand the pity system and true expected value.

🔴 新皮肤溢价期New Skin Premium Period

新英雄/新角色上线时伴生皮肤往往定价偏高，等1-2周活动出来后有折扣或赠送。Launch skins for new heroes/characters are often overpriced. Wait 1-2 weeks for events — discounts or free giveaways usually follow.

🔴 囤积优惠券的心理陷阱The Coupon Hoarding Trap

优惠券不是"省钱"，而是刺激消费。不买立省100%，只买真正需要的东西。Coupons don't "save money" — they stimulate spending. Not buying saves 100%. Only buy what you truly need.
